Charles Lane D. Roches

Behind every great thriller lies a truth that is often simpler, but more powerful: determination. For me, writing is not just a passion; it is the result of a journey built on perseverance.

Inspired by figures of tenacity like Martin St-Louis, I chose to blaze my own trail in the publishing world. Driven by an iron will, I founded LDR Edition to offer readers stories that make no compromises.

A Dark and Authentic Universe

Specializing in psychological thrillers, suspense, and dark romance, my work explores the deepest corners of the human soul. My goal is simple: to keep you captive until the very last line.

The Quebec Signature

As an independent author, I make it a point of honor to preserve the authenticity of our culture. My stories are anchored in a purely Quebecois reality, using the language and expressions that represent us.
